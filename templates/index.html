Dental Caries

Dental caries, commonly known as tooth decay, is a prevalent oral disease characterized by the demineralization of tooth enamel and the formation of cavities.

Gingivitis

Gingivitis refers to inflammation of the gums caused by bacterial infection. Early detection is crucial to prevent progression to severe periodontal diseases.

Mouth Ulcers

Mouth ulcers are painful sores that develop on the mucous membranes of the oral cavity. Causes include viral infections, trauma, or autoimmune conditions.

Tooth Discoloration

Tooth discoloration encompasses changes in the color of the tooth enamel, which can result from extrinsic factors (e.g., foods, beverages) or intrinsic factors.

About

Oral diseases often go undetected, leading to serious health implications. We propose a deep learning approach for oral disease detection using intraoral images, achieving a 94.6% accuracy rate. Our platform aids healthcare professionals and individuals alike in combating oral diseases.
